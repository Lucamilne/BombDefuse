Click on the correct coloured wire as described by the header, e.g. Cut the RGB(255, 255, 255) wire atop the bomb!

Each parameter (red, green, and blue) defines the intensity of the color as an integer between 0 and 255. Should you choose a wrong wire, the bomb will be armed and a countdown will begin. Choose incorrectly again or let the timer run down and it's game over!

You can reset the game, or choose new colours at any time by pressing the red reset button.

Press the grey buttons to change the number of wires connected to the bomb by 3, 6 and 9 respectively. Try 9 for a real challenge!
